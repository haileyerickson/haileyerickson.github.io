Video Producer - Boise State University

My role in producing the seemingly unknown program that Boise State had to offer its students was at first ... intimidating. The program, called Service Learning, let students get a hands-on learning experience to the class's core lessons by doing just that: service. I oversaw and organized the production of documentary-type videos that were geared towards educating students about the program. I assisted in script writing, coordinated schedules between interviewees and community partners, shceduled off-site shooting locations and oversaw filming and post-production editing that eventually led to a major jump in student enrollment and general awareness of the program.
